# Internee.pk — Internship Policies

## Program Overview

Internee.pk is a structured online internship program for students and fresh graduates. The program runs in cohorts and is designed to give you real-world project experience with AI-powered tools and modern web development.

**Program Duration:** 4–6 weeks depending on the selected track  
**Format:** Remote, self-paced with structured task deadlines  
**Tracks Available:** Generative AI, Full-Stack Web Development, Data Science

---

## Deadlines and Extensions

**Standard Policy:**
- Every task has a clearly communicated deadline from the day it is assigned.
- Deadlines exist to simulate a real work environment and to keep cohorts synchronized.

**Extension Policy:**
- You may request **one extension per task**, up to 3 additional days.
- Extensions must be requested **at least 24 hours before** the original deadline.
- To request an extension, message your assigned coordinator on WhatsApp or email `support@internee.pk` with your name, cohort ID, task number, and reason.
- Extensions are not guaranteed — approval is at the coordinator's discretion based on workload and cohort schedule.
- **No extensions are granted after the deadline has passed.**

**Late Submissions:**
- Submissions within 24 hours after a missed deadline incur a 20% score penalty.
- Submissions more than 24 hours late are not accepted unless prior arrangements were made.

---

## Code of Conduct

- All work must be your own. Using AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot) to assist is allowed and encouraged — but submitting fully AI-generated code without understanding it is a violation.
- Plagiarism between interns results in immediate removal from the program.
- Treat all coordinators, mentors, and fellow interns with respect in all communication channels.

---

## Submission Guidelines

- All tasks must be submitted via the official **Internee.pk portal** at `portal.internee.pk`.
- Your submission must include:
  1. A **GitHub repository link** (public or with coordinator access granted)
  2. A **live demo link** (Vercel, Render, Railway, or similar — free tiers are fine)
  3. A **README.md** with setup instructions, screenshots, and tech stack list
  4. A **Loom or YouTube demo video** (2–5 minutes) walking through features

## Certification

Upon successful completion of all tasks with a passing score (≥ 60%), you will receive:
- An official **Internee.pk completion certificate** (digital, shareable on LinkedIn)
- A **LinkedIn recommendation** from your cohort coordinator (merit-based)
- Access to the **Internee.pk alumni network**

Certificates are issued within 7 business days of program completion.

---

## Technical Environment

- You are responsible for setting up your own development environment.
- Free-tier services are recommended: Vercel (hosting), Railway or Render (backend), GitHub (version control), Google AI Studio (Gemini API key — free tier available).
- If you face environment issues (Windows WSL, Node version conflicts, etc.), post in the WhatsApp group — someone has likely solved the same problem.
